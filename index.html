What is Oraichain?

Oraichain is the world's first intelligent and secure solution for emerging Web3, scalable Dapps, and decentralized AI.

  • A secure and fast CosmosSDK-based blockchain with DPoS consensus and optimized sub-networks for AI.
  • Verifiable and trustless AI execution via proof-of-correctness and proof-of-execution.
  • High transaction throughput with optimistic rollups and zk-rollups.

All the necessary information about Oraichain can be found on the website of the project. Go ahead and check it out here. Or ask additional questions on the official telegram channel here. Also, say hi to Duckie, he is the MVP of Oraichain community mods.

Who is Franz Kafka?

He was bohemian novelist, one of the major figures of 20th-century literature. His work fuses elements of realism and the fantastic. It typically features isolated protagonists facing bizarre or surrealistic predicaments and incomprehensible socio-bureaucratic powers. -Wikipedia

Logo of our validator is based on his work "The Metamorphosis".
